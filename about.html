About Me!

Who am I?

I’m a third year Mechatronics Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University, passionate about building integrated systems that blend mechanics, electronics, and software. Through hands-on projects and leadership roles, I’ve developed strong problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills. I’m driven to apply engineering theory to real-world challenges and continue growing as an engineer. My journey in engineering is driven by curiosity to understand how things work under embedded systems and a desire to build the next generation of Robotics.
